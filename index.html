About Design Day

Design Day is a fantastic opportunity to learn from industry proffesionals about modern web design and development! Come join us for a day full of expert speakers and workshops to take your web design skills to the next level! Learn what employers are looking for and network with other like minded creatives. We guarantee you will go home excited and motivated to further develope yourself as a web designer and developer!

Expert Speakers

I have paid experts lots of money to come to this so please buy a ticket so that I can begin to dig myself out of debt! These experts have been designing for decades and have so much knowledge to share with you.

If you consider yourself serious about web design and development, you will not want to miss out on these speaker keynotes. You will be a fool if you do! They have the secrets to success in the field of web design.

These speakers are coming from all corners of the globe to attend. Design Day is truly about bringing creatives together.

What You'll Learn

Responsive Web Design: Make your projects scale beautifully across all devices! PSD to Code: Learn layout strategies to turn your designs into working projects! CSS Animations: Add fun interactions to your projects to enhance user experiences. Bootstrap 4: Learn the basics of this powerful CSS library to make awesome looking projects. Innovative Design Layout: Think outside the box and make unique designs that can enhance any project.
